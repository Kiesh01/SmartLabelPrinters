About US
Smart Label printers Ltd began in 2001 making sticky labels. Starting small, it grew to be one of Kenya's leading label makers. We upgraded from basic machines to advanced ones with special features like drying systems and quality checks. We sell labels locally and in nearby countries like Tanzania and Uganda. Our clients include big names like Kaluworks Aluware and Kenya Sweets Ltd. We have a skilled team who design and improve labels to make them stand out.
Mission
To achieve a stable market leadership in our niche market i.e self adhesive labels with the highest qualitative demands through continual growth
Vision
To convey the message of our customers through labels on their products to our customers' satisfaction
Quality of Service
We pride ourselves on the quality of service rendered to our customers. We have a fluent system for production process under one roof, ensuring that the quality of products manufactured are in close scrutiny at all times by our well trained personel.
We always ensure that orders placed follow the set procedures as when assigned to ensure timely production.Our capability on delivering quality is second to none. Our skilled and well trained personel see to it that this delivery process is as effortless and smooth as possible.
Our existing customers remain confident in our abilties to meet their requirements with the quality of service expected. Our new customers usually join our family of satisfied customers by always returning to us each time to re-order.
